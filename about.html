About Moondoge

MoonDoge is an online magazine speclising in Dogecoin news and articles. Developed by Jakub Minkiewicz (jakubkm.xyz) for a university module.

Dogecoin price data provided by the CoinGecko Public API.
All articles are dynamically rendered, using the Handlebars framework and JQuery.
